Some explanations

Idea of making watches by yourself isn't new:

  • BigTime by Sparkfun (@sparkfun). Interesting project, but, as for me, it isn't tiny and ergonomical (IMHO).
  • Square Arduino watch.
  • 3D-printed watch by МастерКит (привет, Хабр и Geektimes :D).
  • hundreds of other exciting projects as interesting as these above!

It's dull and stupid to copy any of existing stuff, that's why I decided to design everything by myself. Also I didn't want to add DS1307 IC because it's TOO popular and casual.

Why ATmega48?

That chip isn't new, but one day I got unused industrial electricity meter with ATmega48V-10AI. At one moment I wanted to change TQFP32 MCU (9x9 mm) to QFN32 (5x5 mm), however, I had to give up that idea because it's extremely difficult to buy such a chips by one piece.
